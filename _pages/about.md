I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at [Seoul National University](https://en.snu.ac.kr/). I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University under the supervision of [Prof. Soo-Mook Moon](https://altair.snu.ac.kr/smoon/), and I also earned my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University. 

My research focuses on blockchain systems, with particular interests in Layer-1 and Layer-2 scalability, state growth, storage efficiency, databases, security, and trusted execution environments (TEE).
